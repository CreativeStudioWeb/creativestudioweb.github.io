The series

We all agree that community is the most valuable part of every project. We’re a team that’s very passionate about cardano and especially the Cardano community.

We’ve been lucky enough to be a part of many great projects but for quite some time we’ve been thinking about creating something special. A project that’s about giving back, weekly excitement and a strong feeling of belonging.

With our combined experience we promise you that we will deliver a great project that will exceed your expectations and give you something to look forward to at the end of every week! Besides our Discord we’re committing to regular Twitter Spaces and Twitch Q&A’s, here we can come together and discuss the great universe that we’re building.

 Expect a content calendar from us!
