Supernova!
The series

We’ve committed to creating quality art, something original that you haven’t seen yet in the Cardano space. Together with our artists we’ve worked tirelessly for months to create this art from scratch. 

All our characters were firstly hand drawn and generated in 3D. The weapons, armor and features of each Mech were carefully selected from our Supernova series and created as a trait for you to enjoy.

A few blown up GPU’s later, we proudly present to you our Supernova art! As an extra we’ve also recorded the creation of both the NFT collection and the Supernova series. This way we’ll give you a behind the scene look on what it takes to create an original art project.

Of course we also want to show you how much care, love and effort we’ve put into bringing you these amazing Mechs. We truly believe that this will be a piece you’ll be proud of holding.
