In another time, another place, a seemingly endless battle of sorrow and loss ensues for whatever is left of mankind

Humanity lies at the brink of extinction, as a war that ravages the streamline flow of time and space rages forth in a never ending battle for supremacy. Streaks of vibrant Red and White shoot across the stars, concealing Mechas who bear their fangs at each other in order to preserve their own ideals. Their clash is not without strife within, as the two opposing sides each house two factions of varied mindsets underneath.

 Their internal battles go near unseen thanks to the forefront: Angels seeking to restore the fragile balance of all existence, while Reapers are compelled to dominate humanity and its remaining resources. 

Thanks to the abuse of technology and power, the Mechas have now grown a conscience of their own. The unmanned Angels have now chosen to fend off the Reapers while their allied humans gather up the remnants of humanity and potential resources.

Meanwhile, the divided darkness within further their own agendas and it is only a matter of time until another disaster of corrupted origin wreaks havoc across the galaxies
